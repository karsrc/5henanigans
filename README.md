# Shenanigans

![Hackatime Badge](https://hackatime.hackclub.com/api/v1/badge/U08SFQARGGY/karsrc/Shenanigans)

Top-Down PvE game with fun characters.

## Features
- Satisfying combat
- Combo system
- 2 enemies to fight against
- Score System
- Skills
- Great graphic design

## Controls
- 1: Skill 1 (In vessel character its Barrage)
- 2: Skill 2 (In vessel character its Cursed Enhancement)
- 3: Skill 3 (In vessel character its Knife run)
- R: Special Skill (In vessel character its Manji Kick) 
- G: Ultimate (In vessel character its entering The Zone)
- M1: Punch
- Q: Dash
- Shift: Run
- CTRL: Run
- W: Walk forward
- S: Walk Backwards
- D: Walk right
- A: Walk left
- Mouse: Let's the player control the crosshair, which the character stares at

## Skills
- Barrage: Lets out a fury of punches while holding (Skill 1), the moment player lets go of the button, it stops
- Cursed Enhancement: Gives the player a temporary damage buff
- Knife run: Makes the player run in the direction of his crosshair, at the same time dealing damage to enemies standing in his way
- Manji Kick: It's a situational parry, that let's the player get out of a tough situation
- The Zone: Puts the player into a state where every punch results in a black flash, which buffs his damage and returns 0.5 of a heart

## Combo
While punching with M1, each punch deals more and more damage. You are able to switch between enemies and still hit bigger damage, f.ex. if you attack 1 enemy 2 times, and switch to another on the third, the second curse will get the buffed damage from the combo
